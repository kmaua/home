Who am I?

A.K.A. My Origin Story

My name is Kelson Maua. I was born and raised in Dryden, Ontario, and moved to Thunder Bay for school in 2017. Originally I attended Lakehead University, where I studied Computer Science. About halfway through my first year I decided to transfer over to Confederation College to study Interactive Media Design. The more hands-on approach, along with the more media-focused content really drew me to the college, and to my program. I really enjoy learning by doing, rather than learning by listening!

I am currently in the second semester of first year, and I have been a tutor for my Interactive Design Development and Web Design Development classes since October! I love working with anything media related, and my ultimate goal is to land a career in my field that makes me happy and allows me to do what I love!
